One of the most important reasons that President Roosevelt MUST be reelected is the variety of programs he has implemented over the last eight years as a part of the New Deal. It is imperative that you vote for FDR if you want a president who will continue to play an active role in the quality of your everyday life and the betterment of America.

With the impending threat of war with Europe, many of you must be worried about your livelihoods and security in the country. It is important to acquaint yourself with the variety of resources available to you by President Roosevelt.
